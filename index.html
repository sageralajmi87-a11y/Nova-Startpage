How to pin it to your shelf

1

Click "Generate & Open Shortcut Page" — a new tab will open with your custom icon page.

2

In the new tab, click the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top-right corner of Chrome.

3

Go to More tools → Add to shelf (or "Create shortcut" on some versions). Check "Open as window" if you want an app feel.

4

The shortcut appears in your shelf or launcher. Clicking it opens your destination URL with your custom icon!
